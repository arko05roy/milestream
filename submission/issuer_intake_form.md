## 2. Why HACD

- Why should this asset be issued on HACD instead of a normal token contract? Freelancer credentials need anti-spam economics and portable on-chain proof. A plain token mint has zero formation cost and no unique identity per holder.
- What does PoW-backed formation add to this project? Formation cost filters fake profiles. PoW-mined HACD containers make credentials costly to replicate at scale.
- Why does the asset need HACD containers? Each contractor credential is tied to one HACD lot and one unique Contractor ID. Supply is hard-capped at 200 founding contractors.
- What should users remember after seeing this launch? MileStream credentials are formed, not deployed. Your Contractor ID is your HACD name, permanently on-chain.
